About

The layout-align directive takes two words. The first word says how the children will be aligned in the layout's direction, and the second word says how the children will be aligned perpendicular to the layout's direction. Only one value is required for this directive. For example, layout="row" layout-align="center" would make the elements center horizontally and use the default behavior vertically. layout="column" layout-align="center end" would make children align along the center vertically and along the end (right) horizontally.
